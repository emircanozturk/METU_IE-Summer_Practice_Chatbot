CUSTOM STUDENT FAQ DATASET

Q: Is energy production companies (thermal power plant etc.) valid for IE300?
A: No, because energy production companies are similar to continuous production companies, they are not valid. However, it would be better to ask to an advisor.

Q: Is the position that we are employed in the organization important?
A: Yes, it might be important. Therefore, it would be important to ask to an advisor.

Q: If a holiday falls during the internship period, is it counted as a working day?
A: No, public holidays are not considered as working days like the weekends.

Q: Is it necessary to give the required documents face-to-face?
A: No, you can also send them via email or fax.

Q: Where should I submit my documents if I will give them as hard-copy? 
A: You can go to the undergraduate student affairs secretariat in the 1st floor of IE department building.
